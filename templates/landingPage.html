About

AppHello is a web application created by students for students, which can facilitate the decision-making process using the empirical method. Our mission is to help students to choose consciously, comparing courses, exams, universities by entering a few keywords and at the same time reviewing feedback of students.

We strongly believe that AppHello can be leveraged to help students and give them a broader understanding of the educational world.


We would like to make a difficult choice easier.
